About Hasan

Hi, I'm Hasan Al-Rashed, a dedicated Trainee Technical Assistant with a strong passion for media production and broadcast technology. Based in Wembley, London, I bring hands-on experience in technical operations, project coordination, and thriving under the fast-paced demands of production environments.

My Journey So Far:

  • I’ve worked as a Production Runner for renowned clients like Sony Pictures, HSBC, ITVX, and more, gaining expertise in managing equipment setups, troubleshooting technical issues, and facilitating smooth operations.
  • My academic background in Film Studies (2:1) from Queen Mary University of London honed my skills in creative production, visual editing, and scriptwriting, giving me both technical and narrative precision.
  • As an Independent Filmmaker, I’ve directed and edited short films, working closely with sound designers and composers to create compelling stories with a focus on quality and technical excellence.

Skills I Bring

  • Proficiency in tools like Adobe Creative Suite, Davinci Resolve, and IT software for planning and logistics.
  • The ability to troubleshoot technical challenges swiftly, ensuring seamless workflows in high-pressure environments.
  • A collaborative mindset with strong interpersonal and communication skills, making me a reliable team player on any project.

What Drives Me

My fascination with broadcast production stems from its dynamic nature and the evolving role of technology, particularly in live sports coverage. Whether it’s assembling technical setups or exploring innovative production techniques, I enjoy staying at the forefront of industry trends while working on meaningful, high-quality content.
