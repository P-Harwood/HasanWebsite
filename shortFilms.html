Limbo Stretch (2023)

A documentary crew embarks on an intimate and emotional journey as they capture the final days of a man facing the devastating reality of congestive heart failure. Through their lens, they document the raw and unfiltered moments of his struggle, the toll his illness takes on his body, and the quiet resilience with which he approaches the end of his life. Accompanying him on this poignant journey is his exuberant and spirited friend, whose energy and unwavering support bring moments of lightness and hope amid the darkness. Together, their bond provides a glimpse into the power of friendship and human connection, even in the face of mortality. As the crew captures these profound and fleeting moments, they weave a narrative that not only chronicles a life nearing its end but also explores the enduring strength of love, loyalty, and the human spirit.
